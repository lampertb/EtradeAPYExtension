E*TRADE APY Calculator

This extension adds APY (Annual Percentage Yield) calculations to E*TRADE option chains, helping you identify potential returns for both calls and puts.

APY Formula

APY = ((bid price × 10) / (strike price × 1000)) × (365 / days to expiration)

Color Coding

Blue: Highest APY among options with open interest
Green: Positive APY
Red: Negative APY

Additional Indicators

Bold Text: Indicates options that have open interest (actively traded)
Note: APY calculations are automatically updated when option prices change. The extension works with all E*TRADE option table layouts (combined, calls-only, and puts-only).
